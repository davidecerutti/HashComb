How to navigate the HashComb repository: <br>
-Hash-Comb-Java-deprecated -> is the older version of the library, developped in java, acted as aproof of concept for the paper <br>
-Hash-Comb-Python -> is the new usable library, with modern style, flexible and general purpose for HashComb <br>

As a user intrested in *HashComb*, please navigate the Hash-Comb-Python folder. <br>
As a intrested reader of the related paper, to take a look of the proof of concept, please navigate the Hash-Comb-Java-deprecated folder.

If you are intresten on the topic, you can find paper at: <br>
https://arxiv.org/pdf/2406.19418 <br>
https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/stamp/stamp.jsp?arnumber=9733341
